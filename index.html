DeHedge offers an infrastructure that can protect cryptocurrency investors

What is DeHedge?

DeHedge is the world’s first decentralized risk-hedging platform for cryptocurrency investors that offers initial token exchange rate.

Automatic pay out
DeHedge’s smart contracts are programmed to automatically pay out in full in case of an insured event.

DeHedge uses Ethereum which is a public blockchain. The investor can waive an automatic pay out and opt not to make an insurance claim.
Insurance cover does not exceed insurance reserves
DeHedge’s smart contracts use a binary relation algorithm based on insurance reserves and liabilities, thus making it impossible for the latter to exceed the coverage.

How DeHedge Works

We evaluate ICO projects using a unique scoring model developed by DeHedge in collaboration with one of the largest consulting firms in the world (Big Four).

An investor can get insurance coverage for various cases, such as project cancellation or token exchange rate drops.

Step 1: Investments and Insurance
An investor buys ICO A tokens and checks if this ICO is insured at DeHedge.com. If the ICO scored at DeHedge, the investor buys a DeHedge token at its exchange rate and uses it to pay for the insurance coverage. The price is calculated automatically.
Step 2: Automatic Pay Out
In case of an insured event, DeHedge automatically pays out. DeHedge allows insured users to customize their payout settings.
Step 1: Choice and Payment
The investor chooses a traded token, the insurance period, and the range of exchange rate changes covered by insurance. The investor pays for insurance coverage with DeHedge or Ether tokens.

Why DeHedge ICO

Income from the ICO
The size of the insurance reserve depends on the total amount of funds collected from the project. The larger the sum collected, the more tokens are available to be insured by investors.

The structure for managing the insurance reserves generated during the ICO of HedgeCoin tokens:

20%

A permanent reserve for unplanned payouts

80%

Investments into liquid crypto assets to increase the reserves
Up to 20% of tokens issued for insurable projects will be redeemed at a discount for investment purposes. In the future, these tokens will be sold in combination with insurance for the same price as the ICO.

This is the safest way of managing the funds, as DeHedge conducts the independent due diligence review of projects using its own scoring model and can realize assets without any loss in the event of depreciation.
