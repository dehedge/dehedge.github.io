What is DeHedge?

DeHedge is the world’s first decentralised risk-hedging platform for cryptocurrency investors, that insures the initial exchange rate of tokens.

For investors in ICO projects
DeHedge offers insurance against the collapse of a project or a fall below the initial exchange rate for a project token.

If an insured event takes place, DeHedge will pay compensation in exchange for the project token. The maximum loss to the investor will therefore be equal to the cost of the insurance premium.
For cryptocurrency traders
DeHedge offers insurance against falls in the rates of tokens and cryptocurrency traded on the exchanges.

If an insured event occurs, DeHedge will pay the insurance compensation. The maximum possible financial loss to the investor will equal the cost of the insurance premium.

High risks require tools to help investors with hedging. DeHedge provides an infrastructure that protects cryptocurrency investors.

How it works

We evaluate ICO projects using a unique scoring model developed by DeHedge in collaboration with one of the largest consulting firms in the world (Big Four).

An investor can insure themselves against the cancellation of a project or a fall in the exchange rate of tokens.

DeHedge’s smart contracts are programmed to automatically pay out in full in case of an insured event. DeHedge uses Ethereum, a public blockchain.

Why DeHedge ICO

Sale of DeHedge tokens forms a cash reserve of insurance funds. These funds serve as collateral for payments in case of an insured event.

Investment Strategy

Ten billion (10,000,000,000) DeHedge tokens will be issued. The cost of each token will be $0.01 (or the equivalent sum in another currency).

Income from the ICO
The size of the insurance reserve depends on the total funds collected from the project. The larger the sum collected, the more tokens investors can insure.

The structure for managing the insurance reserves generated during the ICO of HedgeCoin tokens:

20%

A permanent reserve for insurance payouts

80%

Investments into liquid crypto assets to increase the reserves
Up to 20% of tokens issued for insurable projects will be redeemed at a discount for investment purposes. In future, these tokens will be sold in combination with insurance for the same price as the ICO.

This is the safest way of managing the funds as DeHedge conducts independent due diligence on projects using its own scoring model and can realize assets without loss in the event of a depreciation.
